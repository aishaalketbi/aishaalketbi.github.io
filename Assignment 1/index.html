30 Minute Film Festival

Dive into a 30-Minute Gateway, Unlocking a Multiverse of Miniature Cinematic Gems!

Purpose of 30MFF

  • Foster creativity and spur rapid ideation among student.
  • Encourage students to think on their feet and adapt to time constraints.
  • Encourage collaboration and exchange of ideas among participants
    through post-screening discussions
  • Inspire students to embrace the art of concise storytelling and effective
    visual communication.

Make a film in 30 minutes!

  • Make a 1.5 minute film (approx 90 seconds)
  • 15 minutes shooting
  • 15 minutes editing
  • Upload it
