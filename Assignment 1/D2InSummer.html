D2 in Summer

D2 in Summer follows A young woman who joyfully heads to D2 as the summer term begins, eager to indulge in her favorite foods. However, when she learns that the formerly lively haven of flavors is closed for the summer break, her excitement swiftly turns to disappointment. The woman is devastated by this shocking turn of events and is left feeling adrift, exposed, and incredibly hungry.
