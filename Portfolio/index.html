I'm Aisha!

A New York Univeristy student based in Abu Dhabi double majoring in Psychology and Interactive Media.

My in-depth knowledge of psychology enables me to approach interactive media from a fresh angle and create immersive experiences that have a tremendous impact on consumers. Through music, I've learned the value of harmony both in sound and in life. It enables me to approach projects with a great awareness of balance and rhythm. Furthermore, I enjoy spending time with my four-legged friends. My dogs bring a sense of playfulness into my life.
